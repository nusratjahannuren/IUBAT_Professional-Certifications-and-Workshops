# Workshop Participation – Challenges in Romantic Relationships and Its Management


## Overview

I had the opportunity to participate in the *workshop on "Challenges in Romantic Relationships and Its Management"* organized by the *Department of Psychology* and *Professor Mahmuda Khanum Healthcare Center* at *IUBAT – International University of Business, Agriculture, and Technology*.

## Workshop Highlights

* Understanding the psychological aspects of romantic relationships
* Strategies for managing common challenges in relationships
* Expert insights on communication, conflict resolution, and emotional well-being
* Engaging sessions with interactive discussions and real-life scenarios

## Skills & Knowledge Gained

* Awareness of relationship dynamics and challenges
* Techniques for effective relationship management
* Insights into mental health and emotional intelligence in romantic contexts


## Application

This workshop enhanced my understanding of interpersonal relationships and equipped me with knowledge to apply psychological principles for better relationship management, personal growth, and emotional well-being.
